About Us
Charotar Education Society was established in the heart of Anand city,

the headquarters of Anand district in AD. It was done in 1916. The constitution of the society was framed by Vitthalbhai Patel of the world famous Bandhu Beldi and the first school of the institution was started D.N. High School.

Keeping in view the day by day increasing demand for female education, the Board of Directors of the Charotar Education Society passed a resolution to establish a separate institution for girls from June 1953 in the general meeting dated: 7-9-1952 to encourage girls' education. The name of the institution was given as "Kasturba Kanya Vidyalaya" in order to associate the name of the late Pujya Kasturba, who inspired millions of women in the independence movement of the country Mahatma Gandhi, the father of the nation of India.
